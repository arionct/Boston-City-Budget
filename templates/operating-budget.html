Expenditures by Department

Overview: The pie chart depicts the FY25 budget allocation among Boston's top 10 departments, highlighting their relative shares in the overall expenditure.

Insights: Boston Public Schools received the largest share of the FY25 budget at 32.9%, amounting to $1,526,629,446, demonstrating a significant investment in education.
