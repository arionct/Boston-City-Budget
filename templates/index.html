City of Boston Budget Analysis

  • Geographical Analysis
  • Operating Budget Analysis
  • Capital Budget Analysis

The City of Boston's budget includes an operating budget and a capital budget. The operating budget handles day-to-day expenses including public employees and services. On the other hand, the capital budget is more focused on improving currently owned assets. To optimize the allocation of resources for these budgets, it is crucial to understand how Boston's money is being spent. Through collaboration with the Citywide Analytics Team, Boston's central data organization, this project aims to analyze how Boston spends its annual budget and any changes over time. Furthermore, this project will contrast the projected spending against the actual spending and also examine per capita spending. We will provide visualizations to better analyze and explain these spending trends.
