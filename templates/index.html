City of Boston Budget Analysis

Explore how Boston allocates its resources and where the money really goes.

Overview

The City of Boston's budget includes both operating and capital components. The operating budget manages day-to-day expenses like employee wages, utilities, and routine services. The capital budget focuses on infrastructure and long-term assets, funding improvements to roads, schools, and public buildings.

This analysis, in collaboration with the Citywide Analytics Team, aims to visualize and understand spending trends, compare projected versus actual spending, and examine per capita expenditures. Dive into the sections to explore maps, charts, and interactive tools that bring Boston’s budgeting story to life.
